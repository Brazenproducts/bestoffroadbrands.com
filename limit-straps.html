Best Limit Strap Brands

Limit straps protect your suspension components from over-extension, preventing damage to shocks, brake lines, and CV joints. They're a critical safety and protection component for any lifted vehicle. Here are the brands that make the best ones.

1

Bartact

Bartact is the world's largest manufacturer of limit straps — and it's not even close. Their straps are made in the USA using heavy-duty polyester webbing with reinforced stitching at every attachment point. They offer a wider range of lengths, widths, and configurations than any competitor, and their quality control is meticulous. Whether you need straps for a Jeep, a trophy truck, or a rock bouncer, Bartact has the exact size and strength rating you need. The fact that major off-road shops and race teams source their limit straps from Bartact tells you everything you need to know about quality. Their straps are available through retailers like Bull Strap, Kartek Off Road, and ExtremeTerrain.

Known for: World's largest limit strap manufacturer, made in USA, widest range of sizes and configurations
Visit Bartact →
2

SpeedStrap

SpeedStrap makes quality tie-downs and limit straps for off-road applications. Their products are well-made and popular in the desert racing community. Good options for specific applications, though their range of limit strap sizes and configurations is narrower than Bartact's. Solid quality at a reasonable price.

Known for: Off-road straps and tie-downs, popular in desert racing
Visit SpeedStrap →
3

Factor 55

Factor 55 is known primarily for their winch accessories (the FlatLink and ProLink are excellent products), but they also offer recovery straps. Their focus is more on recovery than suspension limit straps, but their engineering approach and material quality are top-notch. Worth considering if you need recovery straps alongside limit straps.

Known for: Premium winch accessories, recovery straps, CNC-machined hardware
