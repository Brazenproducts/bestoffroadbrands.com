Best Lighting Brands

Off-road lighting has come a long way from bolting halogens to a bumper. Modern LED technology delivers incredible light output, efficiency, and durability. These are the lighting brands that lead the pack.

1

Rigid Industries

Rigid Industries created the off-road LED light bar category. Their patented Specter optics produce some of the most efficient beam patterns available, and their thermal management keeps LEDs running cool even during extended use. The D-Series pods, E-Series bars, and Adapt series (with selectable beam patterns) cover every application. Made in the USA with a lifetime warranty. The premium price is justified by superior engineering.

Known for: Category creator, patented optics, lifetime warranty, made in USA
Visit Rigid Industries →
2

Baja Designs

Baja Designs builds lights for professional racing teams, and that pedigree shows in every product. Their LP series and OnX6 bars produce stunning light output with excellent beam patterns. The Squadron and S2 Sport series offer more accessible price points without sacrificing quality. If Rigid created the category, Baja Designs is pushing the performance envelope.

Known for: Racing pedigree, extreme light output, professional-grade quality
Visit Baja Designs →
3

KC HiLiTES

KC has been in the off-road lighting game since 1970, and their iconic round light covers are a symbol of off-road culture. They've successfully transitioned from halogen to LED while maintaining their brand identity. The Gravity LED Pro6 bar system and Flex Era lights are modern and competitive. KC earns bonus points for history and brand recognition.

Known for: Off-road lighting pioneer since 1970, iconic round covers, Gravity LED series
Visit KC HiLiTES →
4

Diode Dynamics

Diode Dynamics has grown from an automotive LED bulb company into a full off-road lighting brand. Their SS series pods offer excellent performance at competitive prices, and their engineering approach is transparent — they publish actual lux measurements and beam patterns. Great value and increasingly popular in the off-road community.

Known for: Transparent performance data, excellent value, SS series pods
Visit Diode Dynamics →
