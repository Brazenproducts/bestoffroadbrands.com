Best Suspension Brands

Suspension is the foundation of every off-road build. The right shocks, springs, and control arms transform how your vehicle performs on and off the trail. These are the suspension brands that serious builders trust.

1

Fox Racing Shocks

Fox is the benchmark in off-road suspension. Their shocks appear on everything from stock Ford Raptors to unlimited desert racing trucks. The internal bypass technology, position-sensitive damping, and Live Valve electronic systems represent the cutting edge of shock absorber engineering. Fox invests more in R&D than most suspension companies earn in revenue. The result is a product line that covers every budget and application from bolt-on upgrades to fully custom race builds.

Known for: Racing pedigree, advanced damping technology, OEM partnerships (Ford Raptor, Toyota TRD)
Visit Fox Racing Shocks →
2

King Shocks

Every King shock is hand-built in Garden Grove, California. The level of craftsmanship is extraordinary — each unit is assembled, valved, and dyno-tested by hand. King dominates desert racing and their coilover systems are the go-to for serious off-road builds. Custom valving is available for specific vehicle weights and driving styles. The wait times can be longer than mass-produced alternatives, but the end product is exceptional.

Known for: Hand-built in USA, custom valving, desert racing champion
Visit King Shocks →
3

Icon Vehicle Dynamics

Icon takes a systems approach to suspension — their kits are designed as integrated packages where every component works together. The CDC (Compression Damping Control) valve allows on-the-fly adjustment, which is a game-changer for rigs that see both highway and trail. Made in the USA with excellent engineering. One of the best options for a complete suspension overhaul.

Known for: Integrated suspension systems, CDC adjustable damping, complete lift kits
Visit Icon Vehicle Dynamics →
4

Carli Suspension

Carli Suspension has earned a devoted following by focusing on ride quality for heavy trucks. Their systems are tuned for trucks that carry loads — campers, tools, full beds. Where other brands optimize for show truck heights, Carli optimizes for how the truck actually drives with weight on it. The Ram and Ford Super Duty communities swear by Carli for good reason — these systems genuinely transform how loaded trucks ride and handle.

Known for: Heavy-duty truck suspension specialist, Ram and Ford Super Duty focus, load-optimized tuning
Visit Carli Suspension →
5

Bilstein

Bilstein has been building monotube shock absorbers since the 1950s, and their 5100 series is one of the most popular upgrades for trucks and SUVs. The monotube design provides consistent damping even under heavy use, and the price point is accessible. The 5100 is an excellent first upgrade for stock vehicles, and the 8112 and 8100 series serve more demanding applications. German engineering at its finest.

Known for: Monotube technology pioneer, 5100 series ubiquity, German engineering
